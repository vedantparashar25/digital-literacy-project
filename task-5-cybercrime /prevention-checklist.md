# 🛡️ Cybersecurity Awareness & Prevention Guide

In an increasingly digitized world, maintaining a proactive security posture is essential. Use this checklist to safeguard your digital identity and financial assets.

---

### 💳 Financial & Transactional Security
- [ ] **Verify UPI Requests:** Always double-check "Request Money" vs. "Send Money" notifications. Remember: You **never** need to enter a PIN to receive money.
- [ ] **QR Code Caution:** Avoid scanning random QR codes from unsolicited sources; they can be used to initiate unauthorized transactions.
- [ ] **Secure Networks:** Never perform banking or sensitive transactions on public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks.

### 🔐 Account & Access Protection
- [ ] **Credential Hygiene:** Use complex, alphanumeric passwords and update them quarterly. Avoid using the same password across multiple platforms.
- [ ] **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on all critical accounts (Email, Banking, Social Media).
- [ ] **Trusted Source Installation:** Only install applications from verified repositories like the **Google Play Store** or **Apple App Store**.

### 🌐 Browsing & Communication Safety
- [ ] **Phishing Awareness:** Do not click on suspicious links received via SMS (Smishing), Email (Phishing), or WhatsApp.
- [ ] **Suspicious Calls:** Be wary of Vishing (Voice Phishing). Verify the identity of any caller asking for sensitive personal or financial information.
- [ ] **Metadata Privacy:** Be mindful of the information shared in public forums that could be used for social engineering.

---

### 🚨 Emergency Response Protocols
If you suspect a security breach or have fallen victim to a scam:
1. **Immediate Reporting:** Visit the **National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal** at [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in).
2. **Helpline:** Call the national toll-free helpline at **1930**.
3. **Institutional Contact:** Immediately notify your bank or the service provider to freeze associated accounts.
