# 📱 Social Media Ethics & Professional Conduct Checklist

Maintaining a professional digital footprint is essential for modern career development. Use this checklist to manage your social media presence effectively.

---

### ✅ The "Do’s" (Performance Protocols)
- [ ] **Strategic Thinking:** Always pause and reflect on the potential long-term impact before posting or sharing online.
- [ ] **Professional Branding:** Curate your profiles (Profile Picture, Bio, and History) to reflect a professional identity suitable for your industry.
- [ ] **Digital Respect:** Always maintain a polite and civil tone when interacting with others, even during disagreements.
- [ ] **Credential Integrity:** Use strong, unique passwords for every platform and regularly update them.
- [ ] **Value-Added Content:** Share insights, articles, and updates that provide value to your professional network.

### ❌ The "Don’ts" (Risk Mitigations)
- [ ] **PII Protection:** Never share Personally Identifiable Information (PII) like residential addresses, phone numbers, or ID documents publicly.
- [ ] **Tone & Content Audit:** Avoid posting offensive, polarizing, or unprofessional content that could damage your digital reputation.
- [ ] **Critical Evaluation:** Do not disseminate information without verifying its source; avoid spreading misinformation or "Fake News."
- [ ] **Time Management:** Be mindful of "Doomscrolling"; ensure social media usage does not impede productivity or academic goals.
- [ ] **Conflict Resolution:** Avoid online arguments or "flame wars"; take professional disagreements to private, moderated channels if necessary.

---

### 💡 Key Insight
Your social media presence is essentially a **permanent digital resume**. Ensure it works in your favor by remaining consistent, professional, and secure.
